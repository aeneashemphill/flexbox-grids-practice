Hi! I'm Aeneas.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on storytelling. When I'm not working or developing side projects, I'm hiking, reading, or playing guitar.

I was born in Chicago, raised outside of DC, went to school in Ohio and then New York, and am now back in the DMV. I was raised in a multi-cultural family, my Mom having moved here from India, and it's given me a unique global perspective on everything.

My passion: art. I'm also a professional theater, film, and television writer, and I'm an avid music-follower. My tastes and sensibilities are eclectic and global.

A fun fact: I lived briefly in Rwanda, and was evacuated during the violence in the early 90's. Our journey home and our struggle to bring our Rhodesian Ridgeback home became a big media story, landing our family photo on the front page of the Washington Post.
